Reflective Journal on what I learned in PPW

I. Description

Participating in the Preparation for the Professional World program over the past 14 weeks has been a transformative experience for me. Throughout the program, I focused on enhancing my communication abilities. I had the opportunity to receive valuable suggestions to improve my public speaking and presenting skills. Moreover, I dedicated time to reevaluate my professional letter, email, memo, and business writing skills. The approachable nature of the professors made the lectures entertaining, which ultimately sparked my interest and motivated me to delve deeper into this module. I firmly believe that everything I have learned will play a crucial role in the development of my career as an IT expert.

II. Feelings

Initially, as this module was a non-GPA one, I didn't pay much attention to it. However, my perspective shifted after attending the second lecture. It became evident to me how crucial it is to follow this program. The lectures opened my eyes to the fact that there is always room for improvement and growth. This realization prompted me to change my approach, leading me to pay closer attention to the lectures and actively engage in the homework. As a result, I have witnessed advancements in both my personal and professional abilities.

III. Evaluation

In all honesty, I am grateful that this module was included in this semester's curriculum. It has been instrumental in improving my writing and communication skills. By identifying my weaknesses through the lectures, I have been able to recognize my mistakes and address them promptly before entering the IT industry as a professional. The program has provided me with the tools and knowledge to refine my skills and ensure that I am well-prepared for the challenges that lie ahead.

IV. Analysis

Initially, I lacked enthusiasm for this module as I believed my writing and communication skills were already proficient. However, attending the lectures has taught me that there is always room for improvement, regardless of how confident one may feel. I am grateful for this realization, as it has shifted my perspective and made me understand the importance of continuous growth and development. I firmly believe that the lessons learned from this module will greatly contribute to my career advancement.

V. Conclusion

I am delighted that I made the decision to watch all the lectures offered in the program. They have provided me with invaluable insights into my current areas of improvement. Armed with this knowledge, I can now focus on refining those specific areas and further advancing my abilities. I extend my sincere appreciation to the professors for their guidance and support throughout the program. I am confident that this module has played a significant role in shaping my professional journey, emphasizing the necessity of utilizing my writing and communication skills effectively.

VI. Action Plan

The completion of this module has taught me a valuable lesson: even when we believe we are performing well, there is always room for improvement. Therefore, I commit to carrying forward the lessons learned and not forgetting the valuable knowledge gained from this subject. Moving forward, I will strive to periodically evaluate myself, seeking opportunities to hone my talents throughout my career. I firmly believe that this proactive approach will enable me to continuously grow and achieve my objectives.


In conclusion, participating in the Preparation for the Professional World program has been an enriching experience, particularly in terms of enhancing my communication abilities. This reflective journal serves as a testament to the significant progress made during the program. I am grateful for the opportunity to learn, grow, and refine my skills. I am confident that the knowledge and insights gained will serve as a strong foundation for my future endeavors as an IT professional.

Career Development Plan

My career development plan is to gain extensive experience in various sections of the IT field, avoiding overreliance on a single area.
